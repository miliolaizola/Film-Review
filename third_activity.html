Third Activity

After reading The Godfather answer the questions:

  1. What is the title of the film?
  2. Is it based on a book?
  3. Who is the director?
  4. Who are the stars of the film?
  5. Where is the film set?
  6. When is the film set?
  7. What is the film about?
  8. Which type of film is it?
